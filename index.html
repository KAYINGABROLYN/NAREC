About NAREC

NAREC Girls' Hostel and Conference Centre is a registered business providing hostel accommodation for girls studying at Mengo Senior School. We also offer catering and conference facilities including indoor and outdoor catering services.

Located at Namirembe along Wills Road, Plot 279, we provide a safe and nurturing environment that supports academic excellence and personal growth.

  • Secure 24/7 premises
  • Nutritious meals
  • Study-friendly environment
  • Conference facilities
  • Professional catering services

Our Services

Hostel Accommodation

Comfortable and secure boarding facilities for girls attending Mengo Senior School with all necessary amenities.
